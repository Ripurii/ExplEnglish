EDpuzzle lets you turn any video into an interactive lesson. Crop a clip down to just what you need, then drop in multiple-choice or open questions at specific timestamps -- the video pauses automatically so students have to answer before continuing. You can also add your own voiceover or audio notes over the footage.

It's a great fit for checking listening comprehension without a worksheet: assign a clip, see who watched it and how they answered, and get instant feedback on where the class struggled.
